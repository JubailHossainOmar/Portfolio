Experiences

Responsive Web Design

2021- Present | advance level

Responsive web design is an approach to web design that ensures a website's layout and content adapt fluidly to various screen sizes and devices. With the increasing use of smartphones, tablets, and other mobile devices to access the internet, responsive design has become essential for providing users with a seamless browsing experience across all devices.

Flat Design

2021- 2023 | advance level

Flat design is a minimalist design approach that focuses on simplicity, clarity, and usability. It eschews embellishments such as gradients, shadows, and textures in favor of clean, two-dimensional elements and vibrant colors. Flat design rose to prominence as a reaction to the skeuomorphic design trend, which aimed to mimic real-world objects in digital interfaces.
